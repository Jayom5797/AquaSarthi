The Problem

Water scarcity is a growing crisis in India, particularly in urban institutions like schools and homes for the elderly. Manual monitoring, frequent tank overflows, and undetected leaks lead to significant and unnecessary water loss. These inefficiencies, while small individually, add up to a substantial impact on our precious water resources.

Our Solution

ByteCode introduces an intelligent water management system designed for institutions. We leverage a modern software stack—React, Django, and PostgreSQL—to provide real-time monitoring, data analysis, and actionable intelligence. Our system identifies anomalies like leaks or overuse and sends timely alerts, empowering staff to take immediate action.

Key Features

Backend Architecture

A robust Django backend with a RESTful API handles data processing and anomaly detection, built to scale and integrate with real IoT sensors in the future.

Frontend Dashboard

An intuitive React-based dashboard displays usage trends, reports, and alerts in a user-friendly interface, accessible to non-technical staff.

Simulation Engine

Our current system uses a simulation engine to generate realistic water usage data, allowing us to test and refine our system effectively before full-scale IoT integration.
